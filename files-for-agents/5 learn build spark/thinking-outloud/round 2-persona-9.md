### tldr

We're a social impact startup studio. We pay bounties — small, one-time payments — for people to run tiny social impact business pilots in their communities. The pilots test whether social impact projects can fund themselves through earned income. We measure what works, what doesn't and the blueprint is published under a CC-0 license: no one owns it, anyone can use it. 

We fund local experiments that test whether social impact projects can sustain themselves through real demand (not grants). 

Our long-term goal is to launch social impact startups that can sustain themselves through earned income.

## our model: learn, build and infinite spark


### Learn 

We’ve created an interactive online course on social impact entrepreneurship, and we’ll be sharing case studies that turn real-world experiments into step-by-step blueprints anyone can copy and adapt:

- what the project did
- how many paid hours it created
- what changed for the people involved
- what it cost per outcome
- what didn't work



### Build 

We will be running small pilots in real neighborhoods, measure the impact and generate a transparent and permanent receipt anyone can audit later. 

Each pilot:
- pays real people for real work
- sells a product or service to real buyers
- generates a verifiable impact receipt: who got paid, what it cost, what changed, what failed

**The business is the intervention.** 


### Infinite Spark 

The **Infinite Spark** is what remains after the pilot is over: a tested blueprint plus an economic loop that can keep running without new grants.

We publish every Spark Blueprint under **CC-0**: the business model, the unit economics, the operational steps, the mistakes. Anyone can fork it. Anyone can improve it. The ideas don't belong to us. They belong to whoever runs them next.

We embed a sustainable business model into every projectso funds keep flowing.
When the economic engine works, the impact becomes an **Infinite Fountain**:



### Let's be transparent about the deal:

**What you get:**
- $50–$400 for executing a contained pilot over a few weeks.
- Access to our online course on social impact entrepreneurship.
- A real experience running a social impact business experiment — pricing, selling, delivering, documenting.
- Your work published as a blueprint others can use. Your name on it if you want.

**What we get:**
- Data and learnings from real pilots in real communities.
- Proof (or disproof) that specific business models can sustain social impact.
- A growing portfolio of tested blueprints for our startup studio.
- Our long-term goal is to launch social impact startups that can sustain themselves through earned income.

That's the exchange. We're not disguising it as something else.



### The Bounty Board

We've received seed funding from early partners (names coming soon).

Here are some examples of bounties:

- **Shared Table Night**: host one community dinner with a simple hero product (for example tortillas + salsa), sell tickets plus a small preorder batch to one cafe or office, and give every guest a one-page Impact Receipt showing who got paid and what changed.
- **Flywheel Box**: assemble 30 gift boxes using products from social impact makers, sell them through one company as client gifts, and track how many paid shifts and makers were funded through those orders.
- **Dinner Party Kit**: design a light-touch dinner kit (pre-portioned dry ingredients + recipe + playlist), sell a small run via pre-orders, and document how many people cooked together and how many paid hours the kit created.
- **Commons Table Night**: organize a one-night event with a paid local chef, three short stories from frontline projects, and a printed Commons Receipt at each seat showing wages, materials, and where tonight’s support is going.
- **Infinite Table Chain**: run one link in a global relay — record a 5-minute story + simple recipe, host a small dinner to watch it, fill out a Table Receipt (cost per plate, one outcome, one honest failure), and pass it to the next host.


### Compensation

Each bounty includes:
- **Compensation:** $50 for 3 hours of work.
- **Impact + revenue targets:** number of people reached, hours of paid work created (including your own), revenue from real customers, and one learning question.
- **A market test:** at least one revenue stream must come from a buyer who is purchasing because the product is good — not because the cause is worthy.

**Every idea, every result, every blueprint is published under CC-0.** No one owns the playbook. If these models work, anyone should be able to run them. That's the whole point.


### The Wisdom Council

The wisdom council will serve as mentors in the learning circle and will also help with the voting process.

A small council reviews each bounty:
- Did the pilot actually run?
- Was the impact receipt honest?
- Did the business model show signs of working — or useful signs of not working?
- What did we learn?
